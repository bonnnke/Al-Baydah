Labour & Employment

We as lawyers provides complete range of legal services for labor and employment matters for employers and employees and help them secure their rights in accordance with the applicable local and federal laws of the United Arab Emirates.

Our dedicated team is renowned and reputed for providing practical, innovative advice with well-structured documentation. We offer a variety of legal services for international and regional clients in all sectors.

We provide legal consultation on employment rights and obligations, preparng offer letters, employment agreements and termiation letters, representation before Ministry of labor and labor court, lifting of employment bans, filling of criminal complaints in cases of absconding, settlement of claims for compensation.
